<Section max background={false}>
  ## 3. Purpose of data processing

  ### a) Data collected automatically

  When visiting my website, some technical information is collected automatically:&#x20;

  * IP-Adress
  * Date & time of access
  * Referring URL
  * Browser used & operating system

  This data is analyzed anonymously and is used to improve my website as
  well as security.

  ### b) Cookies (via [Usercentrics Cookiebot CMP](https://usercentrics.com/))

  I use Cookiebot to manage and document your consent for the use of cookies on my website. Cookiebot collects information about your IP address, consent status, and the time of your consent. \
  The processing of data via Cookiebot is based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

  ### c) Contact (via email)

  When writing an email to me, your provided information (such as name, email and message) will be used to respond to you and provide the requested information or assistance. \
  The processing of your data is based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) and my legitimate interest to respond to your inquiries (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
</Section>

<Section max background={false}>
  ## 6. Your rights

  You have the following rights under the GDPR:

  * **Right to Access:** You can request access to the personal data I hold about you.
  * **Right to Rectification:** You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  * **Right to Erasure:** You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  * **Right to Object:** You can object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.
  * **Right to Data Portability:** You have the right to request your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

  To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [work@soren.codes](mailto:work@soren.codes).
</Section>
